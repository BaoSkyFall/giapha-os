# Design System: Tộc Phạm Phú — Tộc Phạm Phú
**Project ID:** 7998967920457136760

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere
Modern Flat Design with Subtle Layers — reflecting Vietnamese cultural heritage. The interface balances traditional warmth (Heritage Red, Prosperity Gold) with modern clarity. Paper-like card surfaces with soft shadows and gold borders evoke physical gia phả genealogy books. Generous spacing ensures readability for family elders. Subtle traditional motifs (lotus, clouds) appear as decorative watermarks.

## 2. Color Palette & Roles
- **Heritage Red** (#B31D1D) – Primary brand color, headers, CTAs, navigation background
- **Prosperity Gold** (#E8B931) – Accent borders, highlights, traditional pattern elements, hover states
- **Jade Green** (#227C44) – Secondary indicators, success states, positive actions
- **Altar Wood** (#3E2723) – Primary text color, deep contrast headlines
- **Accent Pink** (#D46A84) – Female gender markers, subtle accent elements
- **Rice Paper** (#F7F3EA) – Main application background, card surfaces

## 3. Typography Rules
- **Headings:** Playfair Display (Serif) — elegant, traditional feel. Weights: 700 for h1/h2, 600 for h3/h4.
- **Body Text:** Inter (Sans-serif) — highly readable, modern. Weights: 400 regular, 500 medium, 600 semibold.
- **Vietnamese text:** Must support full Vietnamese diacritical marks.

## 4. Component Stylings
* **Buttons:** Rounded corners (8px). Primary buttons use Heritage Red (#B31D1D) with white text. Secondary buttons use outlined style with Heritage Red border. Hover states shift to slightly darker shade with Gold (#E8B931) accent.
* **Cards:** Paper-like appearance with Rice Paper (#F7F3EA) background, subtle Gold (#E8B931) borders (1px), soft drop shadows (0 2px 8px rgba(0,0,0,0.08)). Gently rounded corners (12px). Hover state lifts card with deeper shadow.
* **Inputs:** Light background, Altar Wood text, Gold accent border on focus.
* **Navigation:** Heritage Red background with white/gold text. Active item highlighted with Gold underline.

## 5. Layout Principles
- Desktop-first responsive design
- Centered content with max-width container (1200px)
- Generous whitespace between sections (48–80px vertical spacing)
- Card grids use 3–4 columns on desktop, stack on mobile
- Header fixed at top, Footer anchored at bottom
