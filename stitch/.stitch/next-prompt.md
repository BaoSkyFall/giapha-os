A family media gallery page for "Tộc Phạm Phú" — for preserving precious family photos, documents, and historical records.

**DESIGN SYSTEM (REQUIRED):**
- Platform: Web, Desktop-first
- Theme: Light, warm, heritage-inspired flat design with subtle layers
- Background: Warm rice paper off-white (#F7F3EA)
- Surface: Paper-like cards with subtle gold (#E8B931) borders and soft shadows
- Primary Color: Heritage Red (#B31D1D) for headers, navigation, CTAs
- Accent Color: Prosperity Gold (#E8B931) for borders, highlights, hover states
- Secondary Color: Jade Green (#227C44) for success states and indicators
- Text Primary: Altar Wood dark brown (#3E2723) for headlines and body
- Text on Primary: White (#FFFFFF) on red/dark backgrounds
- Heading Font: Playfair Display (Serif), elegant and traditional
- Body Font: Inter (Sans-serif), clean and highly readable
- Buttons: Rounded corners (8px), Heritage Red primary, comfortable padding
- Cards: Paper-like, gold borders, rounded corners (12px), soft shadows, hover-lift effect
- Layout: Centered content, max-width 1200px container, generous whitespace
- Navigation: Heritage Red background, white/gold text, gold active underline
- Footer: Deep Heritage Red background with gold accents and family motto
- No harsh shadows, warm and inviting — respecting Vietnamese cultural aesthetics

**Page Structure:**
1. **Header (Global):** Heritage Red bar with logo "Tộc Phạm Phú", nav links, and gold CTA.
2. **Title:** "Kho Ảnh Gia Đình" with gold underline, upload button.
3. **Gallery Grid:** Masonry grid of family photos in paper-like frames with gold borders. Category filters for "Tất cả", "Gia đình", "Sự kiện", "Tài liệu".
4. **Photo Lightbox:** Modal view showing enlarged photo, caption, date, and tagged members.
5. **Footer (Global):** Deep Heritage Red with gold accents, three columns, and family motto.
